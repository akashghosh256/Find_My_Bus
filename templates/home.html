Live Location

Live Location allows you to see the real-time location of your bus on a map. This means you'll no longer have to wait at the bus stop wondering when your bus will arrive or if you've missed it. With Live Location, you'll have the power to track your bus and plan your journey accordingly.
To use this feature, simply open Find My Bus. You'll see a live map of your bus route with an icon representing the location of your bus. As the bus moves along its route, the icon will update in real-time to show you its exact location. It's our way of making your daily commute more efficient, convenient, and stress-free.
